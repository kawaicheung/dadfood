Bolognese

A slow-cooked ragù with farfalle

Prep/Cook 30 min Finish 11 min

Gather

  • Farfalle pasta
  • Ground beef
  • Rao's marinara
  • Carrots
  • Celery
  • Mushrooms
  • Shallot
  • Fresh parsley

Prep/Cook — 30 minutes

Step one

Dice the shallot, carrots, and celery into a fine mirepoix.

Step two

Sauté vegetables in olive oil over medium heat until soft.

Step three

Add ground beef and brown, breaking up as it cooks.

Step four

Pour in marinara, simmer on low for 20 minutes.

Step five

Boil farfalle until al dente, reserve a cup of pasta water.

Step six

Toss pasta with sauce, finish with fresh parsley.

Finish — 11 minutes

Plating

Serve in wide bowls, garnish generously with parsley and pecorino.

Wine pairing

A medium-bodied Sangiovese cuts through the richness perfectly.
